<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Miraflores and Huaca Pucllana</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>Love Park:</strong> We will visit the Parque del Amor in the famous Miraflores neighborhood. Opened on Valentine's Day in 1993, it is the perfect place to walk, sit on its mosaic benches, and read phrases dedicated to love.</p>
<p><strong>External View of Huaca Pucllana:</strong> You will appreciate from the outside an impressive view of this pre-Inca pyramidal structure, which stands out in the middle of the city and shows the unique fusion between the past and modern life.</p>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Historic Center of Lima</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>Plaza Mayor of Lima:</strong> Located in the heart of the historic center and surrounded by very important buildings such as the Cathedral, the Government Palace, the Archbishop's Palace and the Church of the Sagrario.</p>
<p><strong>San Francisco Convent:</strong> Very famous for its library and basilica. It is also known for its enormous and mysterious <strong>Catacombs</strong>, where thousands of bone remains from the colonial era rest.</p>
