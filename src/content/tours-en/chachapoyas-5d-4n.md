<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-gray-900 mb-4">Discover Chachapoyas in 5 Days</h2>
<p class="text-lg text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
Five unforgettable days to explore the Amazon, from its viewpoints and archaeological wonders such as Kuélap, Karajía and Revash, to the imposing beauty of the Gocta Waterfall.
</p>
</div>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 1: City Tour and Canyon Route</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p>Pick up from the terminal or airport to your chosen hotel.</p>
<p><strong>10:30 a.m. or 02:30 p.m.</strong> We begin the tour to the viewpoint route. We head to the town of Huancas to visit the Mirador del Huanca Urco, where you can enjoy the landscape; We make a brief stop in the Plaza de Huancas and continue with the visit to the Mirador del Cañón del Sonche, where you can see the Sonche River.</p>
<p>Upon returning we finish the visit at the Mirador of the city of Chachapoyas.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="flex items-center gap-4 mb-4">

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 2: Kuelap, Cable Car and Walking Tour</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>10:00 a.m. Departure towards Kuelap:</strong> Departure towards the Nuevo Tingo district, where there will be a short stop to order lunches. Then they will travel on the first cable car system to the La Malca parador (approximately 25 minutes), where the tickets will be purchased. From there you begin a walk of approximately 30 minutes. until you reach the Kuelap Archaeological Complex, where you will take a tour of approximately 1 hour.</p>
<p>After that, they will enjoy a delicious lunch, then visit the Macro site and return to Chachapoyas.</p>
<p><strong>07:30 p.m. Night Walking Tour:</strong> Departure towards the city viewpoint. We will begin a tour of the main streets of the city, accompanied by a specialized guide who will provide you with historical information. The tour will culminate in a traditional Chachapoyas bar, where you can enjoy a tasting of regional drinks.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-gray-100 p-6">
<div class="flex items-center gap-4 mb-4">
<div class="w-12 h-12 bg-brand-primary text-white rounded-full flex items-center justify-center font-bold text-xl flex-shrink-0">3</div>
<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 3: Quiocta Cavern and Karajía Sarcophagi</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>09:30 a.m. Departure:</strong> We travel from your hotel to the Lámud district, where we will stop to order lunches. Then we will move to the Quiocta Caverns where we can see and photograph large stalactites and stalagmites. After the visit you will enjoy a delicious lunch.</p>
<p>In the afternoon you will visit the Karajía Sarcophagi (a unique funerary pattern in the world located on top of rocky cliffs).</p>
<p>Return to the city between 5:30 and 6:00 p.m.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-gray-100 p-6">
<div class="flex items-center gap-4 mb-4">
<div class="w-12 h-12 bg-brand-primary text-white rounded-full flex items-center justify-center font-bold text-xl flex-shrink-0">4</div>
<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 4: Revash and Leymebamba Museum</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>08:00 a.m. Departure:</strong> We leave for the town center of San Bartolo. From there, we will begin the walk towards the Revash Mausoleums, made up of rectangular funerary houses (chullpas) of 1, 2 and 3 floors embedded in the rock.</p>
<p>In the afternoon, we will travel to the town of Leymebamba, where we will enjoy a traditional lunch. Then, we will visit the Mallqui Center Museum, which houses more than 2,000 objects, 200 mummies and an interesting collection of quipus brought from the mausoleums of the Laguna de los Cóndores.</p>
<p><strong>06:30 p.m.</strong> Arrival at the city of Chachapoyas.</p>
</div>
</div>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 5: Full Day Gocta Waterfall</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>09:30 a.m. Departure:</strong> We will travel to the town of Cocachimba, where we will make a short stop to order lunches and buy tickets. From there we will begin a walk of approximately 2 hours (with the option of renting horses) to reach the second fall of the immense Gocta Waterfall.</p>
<p>At the place, our guide will tell us some legends and stories. There will be enough time to take photographs. After the visit, we will have time for lunch and then we will return to the city of Chachapoyas between 5:30 and 6:00 p.m.</p>
