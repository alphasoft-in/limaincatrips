<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p>Departure from the Lima Inca Trip office - (Lagunas at Av. Padre Lucero N° 1345, Jose Cardenas neighborhood, by motorcycle van and/or motorcycle taxi, to the PV8 - Tibilo Control post, main entrance to the Pacaya Samiria National Reserve), where the visitor is registered by SERNA staff. Departure in well-conditioned canoes, from the PV8-Tibilo port, after three (3 hours) of navigation, we will arrive at the Tranca Flying Post, where we will prepare lunch. After a short rest, we continue navigating to the Poza Gloria Refuge, where we spend the first night. If you decide we can make a night tour, marveling at the first night.</p>

<p><strong>7:00 AM</strong> Breakfast.</p>
<p><strong>7:30 AM</strong> We depart from Posa Gloria by canoe on the Yanayacu River, where we can spot aquatic turtles, a variety of fish, insects, birds and other animals. During the journey to the Armanayacu stream, you will be able to witness the gigantic trees known as the boa tree, pink dolphins, and Paiches.</p>
<p>We will arrive at the Pantheon camp, where we will have lunch; After a stop of approximately 2 hours, we continue to the Camotal control post where we spend the night, after dinner we take a night tour through the Armanayacu stream, where we can see crocodile species.</p>
