<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-gray-900 mb-4">Mysteries of the Mummy Museum</h2>
<p class="text-lg text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
Explore the fascinating Leymebamba Mummy Museum, visit the imposing Kuelap fortress, discover the Karajía sarcophagi, and enjoy a trek to the majestic Gocta Waterfall in this 4-day package.
</p>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 1: Kuelap, Cable Car and Walking Tour</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>10:00 a.m. Departure to Kuelap:</strong> Depart towards towards the Nuevo Tingo district, where a short stop will be made to order lunches. Then you will transfer in the first cable car system to the La Malca stop (approx. 25 min), where the tickets will be purchased. From there you start a walk of approx. 30 min. until reaching the Archaeological Complex, where you will take a tour of approx. 1 hour.</p>
<p>After that, you will taste a delicious lunch, then visit the Macro site and return to Chachapoyas between 5:30 and 6:00 p.m.</p>
<p><strong>07:30 p.m. Night Walking Tour:</strong> Departure towards the Viewpoint of the city of Chachapoyas. From this point, we will begin a tour through the main streets of the city, accompanied by a specialized guide who will provide general information and relevant historical data about the most emblematic places. The tour will culminate in a traditional bar in Chachapoyas, where you can enjoy a tasting of regional drinks, an ideal experience to immerse yourself in the local culture and flavors.</p>
</div>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 2: Quiocta Cavern and Karajía Sarcophagi</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>09:30 a.m. Departure:</strong> We travel from your hotel to the Lámud district, where a stop will be made to order lunches. Then we will transfer to the Quiocta Caverns where we can see and photograph large stalactites and stalagmites. After the visit you will enjoy a delicious lunch.</p>
<p>In the afternoon you will visit the Sarcophagi of Karajía (a unique funerary pattern in the world located high on rocky cliffs).</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-gray-100 p-6">
<div class="flex items-center gap-4 mb-4">

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 3: Revash and Leymebamba Museum</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>08:00 a.m. Departure:</strong> Departure towards the town of San Bartolo. From there the hike will begin towards the Revash Mausoleums, made up of rectangular constructions (Chullpas) of 1, 2 and 3 floors, funerary houses.</p>
<p>In the afternoon you will transfer to the town of Leymebamba where you will taste a traditional lunch. Then you will head to the Mallqui center Museum, which inside keeps more than 2000 objects, 200 mummies and an interesting collection of Quipus, material recovered and brought from the mausoleums of the Laguna de los Cóndores.</p>
<p><strong>06:30 p.m.</strong> Arrival at the city of Chachapoyas.</p>
</div>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 4: Gocta Waterfall Full Day</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>09:30 a.m. Departure:</strong> Depart towards the town of Cocachimba, where a short stop will be made to order lunches and buy tickets.</p>
<p>From there you will begin an approximate 2-hour walk (or you can also rent horses) to reach the second fall of the immense Gocta Waterfall, where the guide will tell you some legends and stories, with time to take photographs. After the visit, there will be time for lunch and then return to the city of Chachapoyas.</p>
<p>Return between 5:30 and 6:00 p.m. approx.</p>
