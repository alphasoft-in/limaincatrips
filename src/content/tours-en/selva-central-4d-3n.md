We visit the Manantial la Virgen, we arrive at the city of Oxapampa where we will visit the Chontabamba district and its tourist attractions such as the Tunki Cueva, where we can observe stalactites and stalagmites, photos with typical houses, visit to the trapiche (warapo) to learn about the brandy process, then we visit the artisan's house where they can put on Austro-German clothing and buy souvenirs, Chontabamba theme park, honey tasting, visit to Tiroles ideal place to taste dairy products and observe the typical Parishpolka dances, visit to the Main Square of Oxapampa and the Santa Rosa church. End of the tour.
