<p><strong>20:45 PM HOTEL PICKUP:</strong> Pick up from your hotel for transfer to the bus terminal (depending on rate: Miraflores, San Isidro, or Barranco).</p>

<p><strong>08:00 AM NAZCA AERODROME:</strong> Passengers must be at the airport and check in at the airline counter.</p>
<p><strong>09:30 AM FLIGHT OVER THE NAZCA LINES:</strong> Approx. Depending on scheduling, the flight begins. In this Aerial Tour in a Cessna Light Aircraft, with individual seats and panoramic windows, you will be able to capture photos and videos of the incredible shapes drawn in the Nazca desert. These famous lines were declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1994, due to their mysterious origin, which causes great intrigue to millions of people around the world.<br><br>The aircraft will pass over each figure twice, so that passengers on both sides of the plane can appreciate them well and take photos of the figures, a slight turning movement will even be made for better views. Some of the figures you will see include the whale, the trapezoid, the monkey, the dog, the condor, the spider, the hummingbird, the pelican, the parrot, the tree, and the hands. The exciting flight will last 30 minutes.<br><br>After the flight, pick up from the aerodrome and transfer to the terminal in Nazca. Free time for lunch near the area.</p>
<p><strong>14:30 PM ARRIVAL AT TERMINAL:</strong> You must present yourself at the Cruz del Sur Terminal in Nazca.</p>
<p><strong>15:00 PM DEPARTURE TO LIMA:</strong> Departure of the bus from the Cruz del Sur terminal to Lima (passenger must be at the terminal 45 minutes before).</p>
<p><strong>22:30 PM ARRIVAL IN LIMA:</strong> Approx. Arrival at the Cruz del Sur terminal in Lima. Only in rate with pickup, includes transfer to your hotel in Lima. END OF SERVICES.</p>
