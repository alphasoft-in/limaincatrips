<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-gray-900 mb-4">The Great Vilaya Experience</h2>
<p class="text-lg text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
Embark on a fascinating 3-day trek through the Gran Vilaya Valley, discovering remote archaeological sites of the Chachapoyas culture hidden in the cloud forest, concluding with a visit to the majestic Kuelap Fortress.
</p>
</div>

<div class="bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-gray-100 p-6">

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 1: Chachapoyas - Huaylla Belén - Congón</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>07:00 a.m.</strong> Departure from Chachapoyas in private transport to the Utcubamba Valley, ascending to the community of Karajía, and continuing to the beautiful Huaylla Belén valley (approx 3 hours).</p>
<p>From here, we start our downhill trek for approximately 4 to 5 hours, passing through different ecological levels and cloud forests.</p>
<p>We arrive at the community of Congon, where we will have dinner and spend the night in a basic rural hostel.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-gray-100 p-6">
<div class="flex items-center gap-4 mb-4">
<div class="w-12 h-12 bg-brand-primary text-white rounded-full flex items-center justify-center font-bold text-xl flex-shrink-0">2</div>
<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 2: Congon - Lanche - Yumal</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p>After breakfast, we begin an uphill hike for about 3 hours to the Lanche pass.</p>
<p>Along the way, we will observe typical flora and fauna of the cloud forest, such as orchids and bromeliads.</p>
<p>We continue the hike (about 3 hours more) towards the community of Yumal, passing near the archaeological site of Pirquilla (observation from a distance).</p>
<p>Dinner and overnight in a basic rural hostel in Yumal.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-gray-100 p-6">
<div class="flex items-center gap-4 mb-4">
<div class="w-12 h-12 bg-brand-primary text-white rounded-full flex items-center justify-center font-bold text-xl flex-shrink-0">3</div>
<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 3: Yumal - Kuelap - Chachapoyas</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p>Early breakfast to start our 2-hour hike to the imposing Kuelap Fortress.</p>
<p>Guided tour of approximately 2 hours inside the archaeological complex, exploring its circular houses, high walls, and ceremonial centers.</p>
<p>After the visit, we walk down to the village of Tingo (or descend by cable car, optional not included) where we will have lunch.</p>
<p>Return in our private transport to Chachapoyas, arriving around 5:00 p.m.</p>
</div>
