<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">

<p><strong>Ballestas Islands:</strong> We arrive in Paracas and head to the Chaco port. Visit the Ballestas Islands on a 1 hour 30 minute tour, where we will visit the Paracas Reserve and appreciate the marine flora and fauna. Then we will taste the typical sweets of Paracas: Chocotejas, Cookies, tejas, among others.</p>
<p><strong>Overnight in Paracas.</strong></p>
