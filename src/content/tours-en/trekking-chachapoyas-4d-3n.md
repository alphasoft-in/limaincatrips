</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>08:30 a.m.</strong> Departure to the village of Cocachimba. From here we begin a 2 to 2.5-hour hike through the cloud forest to the base of the Gocta Waterfall, the third highest free-falling waterfall in the world.</p>
<p>Time to enjoy the landscape, take photos, and relax in the surrounding nature.</p>
<p>Hike back to Cocachimba for lunch and return to Chachapoyas.</p>
