<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-gray-900 mb-4">The Mystery of the Mummies</h2>
<p class="text-lg text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
An extreme expedition for true adventurers. We will hike through dense cloud forests and muddy trails to reach the remote Laguna de los Cóndores, where hundreds of Chachapoyas mummies were discovered on cliffs overlooking the dark waters.
</p>
</div>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 1: Chachapoyas - Leymebamba - Laguna de los Cóndores</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>06:00 a.m.</strong> Departure from Chachapoyas to the town of Leymebamba (approx 2 hours).</p>
<p>In Leymebamba, we meet our local guides and muleteers. After breakfast, we start our demanding hike.</p>
<p>The trek takes between 8 and 10 hours, crossing mud trails, paramo ecosystems, and cloud forests, reaching altitudes over 3,500 masl.</p>
<p>Late arrival at the rustic cabin next to the Laguna de los Cóndores for dinner and rest.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-gray-100 p-6">
<div class="flex items-center gap-4 mb-4">
<div class="w-12 h-12 bg-brand-primary text-white rounded-full flex items-center justify-center font-bold text-xl flex-shrink-0">2</div>
<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 2: Exploration of the Mausoleums and Lagoon</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p>After breakfast, we board a rowboat to cross the dark waters of the lagoon.</p>
<p>We disembark and hike through thick vegetation for about 1 hour to reach the cliffs where the Chachapoyas mausoleums are located.</p>
<p>We will observe the tombs, some still containing remains and offerings, while our guide explains the history and discovery of the site.</p>
<p>Return to the cabin for lunch. Afternoon free to fish or rest. Dinner and overnight stay.</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-gray-100 p-6">
<div class="flex items-center gap-4 mb-4">
<div class="w-12 h-12 bg-brand-primary text-white rounded-full flex items-center justify-center font-bold text-xl flex-shrink-0">3</div>
<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 3: Return to Leymebamba - Chachapoyas</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p>Early breakfast to begin the long hike back to Leymebamba (8 to 9 hours).</p>
<p>Upon arriving in Leymebamba, we will visit the impressive Mallqui Center Museum, which houses the mummies rescued from the lagoon.</p>
<p>After the visit, our transportation will take us back to Chachapoyas, arriving around 8:00 p.m.</p>
