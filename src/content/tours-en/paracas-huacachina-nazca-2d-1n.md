<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 1: Ballestas Islands - Winery - Huacachina</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p>Embark on the speedboat and explore the best of marine life! Have fun with seals, sea lions, Humboldt penguins, and seagulls as they show off in a maritime spectacle you can't miss. A wonderful experience for ocean lovers!</p>
<p><strong>Ballestas Islands:</strong> We arrive in Paracas and head to the Chaco port. Visit the Ballestas Islands on a 1 hour 30 minute tour, where we will visit the Paracas Reserve and appreciate the marine flora and fauna. Then we will taste the typical sweets of Paracas: Chocotejas, Cookies, tejas, among others.</p>
<p><strong>Wine Tasting:</strong> To the rhythm of poems, we will taste the best wines, piscos, and creams of Ica. We will enjoy the atmosphere of the winery and have time for photos and shopping.</p>
<p><strong>Huacachina:</strong> We continue the tour and arrive in Huacachina, one of the most fun attractions in the southern area. You will discover an oasis in the middle of the desert, with a magnificent mermaid history.</p>
<p><strong>Dune Buggies and Sandboarding:</strong> We will hop on the dune buggies, where for about 40 minutes you will fully enjoy an extraordinary adventure.</p>
<p>Tour ends in Huacachina. <strong>Overnight in Ica.</strong></p>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 2: Nazca - Lima</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>05:00 AM. Departure to Nazca:</strong> Pickup from Ica and transfer to the aerodrome.</p>
<p><strong>08:00 AM. Approx. Flight over the Nazca Lines:</strong> Arrival in Nazca. In this aerial tour in a Cessna light aircraft, with individual seats and panoramic windows, you will be able to capture in photos and videos the incredible shapes drawn in the Nazca Desert. These famous lines have been declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO since 1994, due to their mysterious origin, which causes great intrigue to millions of people around the world.</p>
<p>The aircraft will pass twice over each figure so that passengers on both sides of the plane can appreciate them well and take photos of the figures, even making a slight turning movement for better views. Some of the figures you will see include the whale, the trapezoids, the monkey, the dog, the condor, the spider, the hummingbird, the pelican, the parrot, the tree, and the hands. The thrilling flight will last 30 minutes.</p>
<p><strong>Transfer to Huacachina:</strong> Pickup from the aerodrome and transfer to Huacachina.</p>
<p><strong>15:30 PM:</strong> Pickup from Huacachina.</p>
<p><strong>22:00 PM:</strong> Approximate arrival in Lima. End of services.</p>
