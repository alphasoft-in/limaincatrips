<p class="text-lg text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 1: Kuelap and Cable Car</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>10:00 a.m. Departure to Kuelap:</strong> We will head to the district of Nuevo Tingo, where we will make a short stop to order our lunches.</p>
<p>Then we will transfer in the first cable car system in Peru to the La Malca stop (approx. 25 min), where the tickets will be purchased. From there we start a walk of approx. 30 min. until we reach the Kuélap Archaeological Complex, where we will take a tour of approx. 1 hour.</p>
<p>After that, we will taste a delicious lunch, visit the site of Macro and return to Chachapoyas.</p>
<p><strong>05:30 - 06:00 p.m.</strong> Return to the city.</p>
</div>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 2: Gocta Waterfall Full Day</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>09:30 a.m. Departure:</strong> We will travel to the town of Cocachimba, where a short stop will be made to order lunches and buy tickets.</p>
<p>From there a hike of approximately 2 hours will begin (or you can also rent horses) to reach the second fall of the immense Gocta Waterfall, a place where the guide will tell you some legends and stories. There will be enough time to take pictures.</p>
<p>After the visit, we will have time for lunch and then we will begin our return to the city of Chachapoyas.</p>
<p><strong>05:30 - 06:00 p.m.</strong> Return to the city and end of our services.</p>
