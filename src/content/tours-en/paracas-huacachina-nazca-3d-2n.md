Discover the best of Paracas, Huacachina, and Nazca on this 3-day, 2-night tour. Enjoy marine wildlife, desert adventure, and the mystery of the Nazca Lines, with options for a private or shared visit to the Paracas National Reserve.

<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-brand-primary mb-6">Option 1: Paracas National Reserve in Private</h2>

<div class="space-y-6 mb-8">
<div class="bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-gray-100 p-6">
<div class="flex items-center gap-4 mb-4">
<div class="w-12 h-12 bg-brand-primary text-white rounded-full flex items-center justify-center font-bold text-xl flex-shrink-0">1</div>
<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 1: Ballestas Islands - Reserve</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>Pickup from Lima:</strong> 05:20 AM Approx. Brief stop en route for breakfast (free time).</p>
<p><strong>08:30 AM Ballestas Islands:</strong> We arrive in Paracas and head to the Chaco port. Visit the Ballestas Islands on a speedboat, in a 1 hour 30-minute tour where we will visit the Ballestas Islands Reserve. You will be able to observe the Candelabra Geoglyph, marine geoforms, and spot marine flora and fauna such as sea lions, Humboldt penguins, and seagulls as they show off in a maritime spectacle you can't miss.</p>
<p><strong>10:30 AM Paracas Boulevard:</strong> After our boat ride, we will stroll along the Paracas Boulevard, where we will taste typical Paracas sweets: Chocotejas, Cookies, tejas, among others.</p>
<p><strong>11:30 AM Paracas Terrestrial Reserve (RNP):</strong> We begin the overland tour of the RNP. We will take a tour of the Paracas Peninsula making stops to observe the colorful reddish and yellowish desert. We will visit the La Cathedral Viewpoint, an imposing rock formation caused by sea and wind erosion over the centuries. We continue to the Isthmus Viewpoint which offers stunning views of the Paracas peninsula and Yumaque Beach, where we will observe migratory birds, marine fossils, turritellas, and more. Next, we will visit the Red Beach, which offers an impressive landscape with a red color contrast between the coastal desert and the sea. Then we will visit the Supay beach viewpoint, with dark sand tones, from gray to black, making a great contrast with the bright yellow cliff and the sea. After the tour, we arrive at Lagunilla beach, where all the beachfront restaurants are located. Free time for lunch. Our last visit will be La Mina beach, famous for its cold and crystal-clear waters, where you will have 25 min. of free time to walk around the beach or swim. The tour ends with a return to your hotel.</p>
<p><strong>Overnight at your chosen hotel in Paracas.</strong></p>
</div>
</div>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 2: Ica - Huacachina</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p>Breakfast at the hotel. Pickup at the El Chaco port to start the tour to Ica.</p>
<p><strong>12:30 PM Winery in Ica:</strong> Arrival at the winery in Ica for an oenological tour where you can learn about the elaboration and production of wines, piscos, and creams from Ica. We will enjoy the atmosphere of the winery.</p>
<p><strong>13:00 PM:</strong> Time for lunch.</p>
<p><strong>14:30 PM Huacachina Oasis:</strong> We continue the tour and arrive at La Huacachina, one of the most fun attractions in the southern area. You will discover an oasis in the middle of the desert, with a magnificent mermaid history.</p>
<p><strong>Dune Buggies and Sandboarding:</strong> We will hop on the dune buggies, where for about 40 minutes you will fully enjoy an extraordinary adventure. Once deep into the dunes, we will be able to do the famous sandboarding, sliding on a board and taking pictures from its viewpoint. Free time at the oasis for shopping and photos.</p>
<p>Tour ends in Huacachina. <strong>Overnight at your hotel in Ica.</strong></p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="bg-white rounded-xl shadow-sm border border-gray-100 p-6">
<div class="flex items-center gap-4 mb-4">
<div class="w-12 h-12 bg-brand-primary text-white rounded-full flex items-center justify-center font-bold text-xl flex-shrink-0">3</div>
<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 3: Nazca - Lima</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>05:00 AM Departure to Nazca:</strong> Pickup from Ica and transfer to the aerodrome.</p>
<p><strong>09:30 AM Approx. Flight over the Nazca Lines:</strong> In this aerial tour in a Cessna light aircraft, with individual seats and panoramic windows, you will be able to capture in photos and videos the incredible shapes drawn in the Nazca Desert. These famous lines have been declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO since 1994, due to their mysterious origin, which causes great intrigue to millions of people around the world. The aircraft will pass twice over each figure so that passengers on both sides of the plane can appreciate them well and take photos of the figures, even making a slight turning movement for better views. Some of the figures you will see include the whale, the trapezoid, the monkey, the dog, the condor, the spider, the hummingbird, the pelican, the parrot, the tree, and the hands. The thrilling flight will last 30 minutes.</p>
<p><strong>Transfer to Huacachina:</strong> Pickup from the aerodrome and transfer to Huacachina.</p>
<p><strong>15:30 PM:</strong> Pickup from Huacachina.</p>
<p><strong>22:00 PM:</strong> Approximate arrival in Lima. End of services.</p>
