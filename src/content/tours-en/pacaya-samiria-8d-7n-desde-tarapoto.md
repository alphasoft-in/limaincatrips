<p class="text-lg text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
The "Pacaya Samiria National Park" is known for being one of the most naturally beautiful areas of the country. The objective of this Tour in Pacaya-Samiria is for you to discover the spectacular natural diversity of flora and fauna, the magnificent wildlife and the history of the region.
</p>
<p class="text-lg text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
It will be an unforgettable experience in a twelve-hour trip navigating the Amazon River, which will recall the adventures of the first European navigators through the Amazon jungle, or the pages of Love in the Time of Cholera by Garcia Marquez. This trip will take us to the community of Lagunas, located right next to the National Park of more than 2000 km2.
</p>
<p class="text-lg text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
During the trip and upon arriving at the reserve, we will see squirrel monkeys, a large number of birds and animals, as well as plants of peculiar beauty. Our trip is also culture, you will hear legends of old hunters next to campfires, you will see, enjoy and understand our millenary dances and learn the history of our Amazonian peoples. If you want to know the real jungle, this tour is for you!
</p>
