</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>Breakfast at the hotel.</strong></p>
<p>Transfer to the picturesque town of Cocachimba. From here, we will begin a trek through a cloud forest, observing diverse flora and fauna, until reaching the base of the Gocta Waterfall (771 masl).</p>
<p>Enjoy the immense waterfall and return to Cocachimba for lunch.</p>
<p>Overnight in Chachapoyas.</p>
