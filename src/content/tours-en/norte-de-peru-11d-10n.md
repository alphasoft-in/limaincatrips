</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 2: Huacas de Moche, Chan Chan and Huanchaco</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>Breakfast at the hotel.</strong></p>
<p>In the morning, visit the Huacas del Sol and de la Luna, impressive mud pyramids of the Moche culture. We will appreciate their original colorful murals.</p>
<p>Free time for lunch in Huanchaco, a traditional fishing resort, famous for the "Caballitos de Totora".</p>
<p>In the afternoon, visit the largest mud citadel in the Americas: Chan Chan, former capital of the Chimú Empire.</p>
<p>Overnight in Trujillo.</p>
</div>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 3: El Brujo Complex and travel to Chiclayo</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>Breakfast at the hotel.</strong></p>
<p>Departure to the El Brujo Archaeological Complex. Visit the Cao Museum, which houses the famous mummy of the Lady of Cao, evidence of matriarchy in pre-Columbian cultures.</p>
<p>Continuation of the journey to Chiclayo (approx. 3 hours).</p>
<p>Arrival, transfer to the hotel and overnight in Chiclayo.</p>
</div>
</div>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 4: Lord of Sipán and Pyramids of Túcume</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>Breakfast at the hotel.</strong></p>
<p>Excursion to Huaca Rajada, discovery site of the tomb of the Lord of Sipán. Visit to the Site Museum.</p>
<p>Transfer to the Valley of the Pyramids of Túcume, ceremonial center of the Lambayeque culture. Visit to its site museum and viewpoint.</p>
<p>In the afternoon, visit the Royal Tombs of Sipán Museum in Lambayeque, one of the most impressive museums in South America.</p>
<p>Overnight in Chiclayo.</p>
</div>

<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">

<p>Free afternoon for shopping or resting.</p>
<p>Overnight in Chiclayo.</p>
