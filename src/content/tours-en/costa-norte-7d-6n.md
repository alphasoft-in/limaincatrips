</div>

</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>Breakfast at the hotel.</strong></p>
<p>Visit to Chan Chan, the largest mud city in America and former capital of the Chimú empire. We will tour the Nik-An Palace, its squares and rooms decorated with marine reliefs.</p>
<p>Free afternoon for handicraft shopping or personal activities.</p>
<p>Overnight in Trujillo.</p>

</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">

<p>Departure to the Chicama valley to visit the El Brujo Archaeological Complex and the Cao Museum, home of the mummy of the Lady of Cao.</p>

</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
