<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>07:30 am - 08:00 am:</strong> Pick up from the Nazca Bus Station or direct arrival of passengers at the Maria Reiche Airport.</p>
<p><strong>Overflight of the Nazca Lines:</strong> In this Air Tour in a Cessna Light Aircraft, with individual seats and panoramic windows you will be able to capture in photos and videos the incredible shapes traced in the Nazca desert. These famous lines have been declared a World Heritage Site by UNESCO since 1994, due to their mysterious origin, which causes great intrigue to millions of people around the world.</p>
<p>The aircraft will pass 2 times over each figure, so that passengers on both sides of the plane can appreciate them well and take photos of the figures, even a slight rotary movement will be made to get better views. Some of the figures you will see include the whale, the trapezoid, the monkey, the dog, the condor, the spider, the hummingbird, the alcatraz, the parrot, the tree, and the hands. The exciting flight will last 30 minutes.</p>
<p><strong>Return:</strong> Transfer to the Nazca Bus Station. END OF SERVICES.</p>
