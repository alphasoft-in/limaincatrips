</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 4: Kuélap and Cable Car</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>09:45 a.m.</strong> Get on the cable car in El Tingo and let yourself be amazed by the spectacular landscapes. Visit the mysterious archaeological complex of Kuélap, the imposing fortress of the Chachapoyas culture, and immerse yourself in its fascinating history.</p>
<p>At <strong>05:30 p.m.</strong>, return to the city with unforgettable memories of a day full of adventures.</p>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 5: Full Day Gocta Waterfall</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p>Departure to the town of Cocachimba, where a short stop will be made to order lunches and buy tickets.</p>
<p>From there, a walk of approximately 2 hours will begin (or you can also rent a horse) to reach the second fall of the immense Gocta Waterfall, where the guide will tell you some legends and stories. Enough time will be given for taking photographs.</p>
<p>After the visit you will enjoy a delicious lunch and then we will start the return to the city of Chachapoyas.</p>
</div>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 6: Full Day Karajía Sarcophagi and Return to Tarapoto</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p>Departure to the district of Lámud, a place where a stop will be made to order lunches. Then we will move to the viewpoint of the Utcubamba canyon, we will return to Lámud and taste a delicious lunch.</p>
<p>In the afternoon you will visit the mysterious Karajía Sarcophagi (a funeral pattern unique in the world anchored at the top of cliffs).</p>
<p>At the end of the afternoon, return to the city of Chachapoyas and boarding towards Tarapoto.</p>
