<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-gray-900 mb-4">Huaylla Belén, Luya, Amazonas, Peru</h2>
<p class="text-lg text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
If you are a nature lover, Huaylla Belén is a surprising valley to enjoy beautiful Andean landscapes, we can appreciate the cloud forest and the exuberant wildlife that surrounds it. This beautiful natural landscape harmonizes with one of the most important funerary patterns of the Chachapoyas Culture, which are the Karajía Sarcophagi, located in the province of Luya.
</p>
<p class="text-lg text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
These pre-Inca constructions are considered unique in the world due to their particular anthropomorphic style and the type of construction nestled in the rocky cliffs. These funerary sphinxes are located at the top of a rocky limestone mountain and preserve their original state to this day, dating from the years 1100 to 1460 AD. Without a doubt, it is one of the most important tourist attractions in the Amazon Region that you should not miss.
</p>

<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p>Our tour begins at <strong>7:00 AM</strong> with a trip through the Utcubamba River Canyon. The landscapes are definitely impressive. Upon reaching the Utcubamba Canyon, we will ascend to the City of Luya, and then continue our journey towards the Huaylla Belén valley.</p>
<p>At <strong>10:30 AM</strong>, our guide will show us and explain everything about the nature of the place. We will visit the valley for 2 hours, ending with a box lunch (ideal for the place), and then travel in a van for an hour to the Karajía Sarcophagi.</p>
<p>At <strong>12:30 PM</strong> We move to the Community of Cruzpata, where we leave our mobility and walk 40 minutes (a distance of 2 kilometers) to reach the Karajía Sarcophagi, imposing anthropomorphic structures that date back to approximately the year 1100. For this section, it is recommended to rent horses, especially for the return. There our guide will explain everything about the place to us for a reasonable amount of time and then return to Cruzpata.</p>
<p>At <strong>3:00 PM</strong> We board our mobility to return to the city of Chachapoyas, arriving at the <strong>5:00 PM</strong> approximately.</p>
