Our day begins by visiting the historic Kimiri suspension bridge, a cultural heritage of the nation, then we will have a panoramic view of the great Profile of the Sleeping Native, followed by a dip in the best waterfalls in the area Bayoz and Velo de la Novia, a visit to the port of Yurinaki where we will delight ourselves with Amazonian gastronomy, then we will visit the Ashaninka native community to learn about their customs and traditions, we will put on their traditional clothes and dance around a campfire, visit the port of Yurinaki where we will delight ourselves with Amazonian gastronomy and we will also visit one of the industrial companies of Chanchamayo to taste coffee and other products of our province. End of the tour.

Our day begins by touring the beautiful green mantle of the Central Jungle and our first stop is at the Paucartambo bridge, the border between the Junin and Pasco regions, arrival at the Churumazu sector to practice canopy (optional). We visit the Manantial la Virgen, we arrive at the city of Oxapampa where we will visit the Chontabamba district and its tourist attractions such as the Tunki Cueva, where we can observe stalactites and stalagmites, photos with typical houses, visit to the trapiche (warapo) to learn about the brandy process, then we visit the artisan's house where they can put on Austro-German clothing and buy souvenirs, Chontabamba theme park, honey tasting, visit to Tiroles ideal place to taste dairy products and observe the typical Parishpolka dances, visit to the Main Square of Oxapampa and the Santa Rosa church.
