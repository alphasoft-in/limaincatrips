<p class="text-lg text-gray-700 leading-relaxed">
The Pacaya Samiria National Reserve is known for being one of the areas of the country of greatest natural beauty. The objective of this Tour to Pacaya-Samiria is for you to discover the spectacular natural diversity of flora and fauna, the magnificent wildlife and the history of the region. If you are looking to know the real jungle, this tour is for you!
</p>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 1</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>4:00 AM</strong> Staff from our agency will pick you up at your hotel to transfer you to the port of the city of Yurimaguas. We then start our 4-hour river transport trip to the community of Lagunas, where we meet with our native guides. After breakfast we start our 30-minute transfer by motorcycle taxi to Tibilo port.</p>
<p><strong>10:00 AM</strong> We start our expedition, this day we will use artisanal boats to move for 5 hours along the Tibilo river to our cabin located in the sector called Posa Gloria. During our visits we will do endemic bird watching, crocodile sighting, piranha fishing among other activities.</p>
</div>

<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 2: Pose Gloria – Panteon</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>8:00 AM</strong> After breakfast, we start the day in a canoe to get around during the excursion. This day we will do crocodile sighting, monkeys, exotic Amazonian birds, piranha fishing among other activities. During the afternoon we will have a 2-hour trekking, where we will learn about medicinal plants and giant trees.</p>
<p><strong>7:00 PM</strong> We start our night visit to sight crocodiles, fish for piranhas and enjoy Amazonian legends and the peace that nature brings us.</p>
</div>

</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>8:00 AM</strong> We start our 4-hour trip to the point called Camotal, during our tour we can appreciate dolphins, river otters, monkeys and aquatic birds. Lunch in Camotal, in the afternoon you will have free time so you can swim and enjoy the peace that nature brings us.</p>
