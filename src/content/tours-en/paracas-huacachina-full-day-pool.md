<h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900">Day 1: Paracas, Ballestas Islands and Huacachina Oasis</h3>
</div>
<div class="text-gray-600 space-y-2">
<p><strong>MORNING - LIMA TO PARACAS:</strong> Pick-up from the indicated location in Lima and transfer to Paracas in tourist transportation.</p>
<p><strong>BALLESTAS ISLANDS:</strong> We begin the tour by visiting the BALLESTAS ISLANDS, on a 1 hour and 30-minute excursion by speedboat, where we will visit the Paracas Reserve and appreciate the marine flora and fauna, as well as the famous Candelabro geoglyph. Then we will taste the typical sweets of Paracas: chocotejas, cookies, tejas, among others.</p>
<p><strong>WINE TASTING IN ICA:</strong> We arrive at the estate in Ica where we will have a WINE TASTING. To the rhythm of poems, we will taste the best wines, piscos, and creams from Ica. We will enjoy the atmosphere of the wine winery and have time for photos, lunch, and shopping.</p>
<p><strong>HUACACHINA OASIS:</strong> We continue the tour and arrive at Huacachina, one of the most entertaining attractions in the southern area. You will discover an oasis in the middle of the desert, with a magnificent legend of mermaids.</p>
<p><strong>DUNE BUGGIES & SANDBOARDING:</strong> We will board the dune buggies, also known as sand cars, where for approximately 45 minutes you will fully enjoy an extraordinary adventure riding through the desert dunes and sliding down on sandboards.</p>
<p><strong>04:15 PM Approx:</strong> Departure from Huacachina and return to Lima.</p>
<p><strong>10:00 PM Approx:</strong> Arrival in Lima and transfer to the initial pick-up point.</p>
